Each circle is an experiment involving a nature-based engineering method. Its results may be significant or not significant. Each experiment applies one of the two treatments: deforestation or afforestation. The experiments measure several properties of the watersheds after the treatment was applied. Pick the measurement to display on the right.
In the final version, the circle colors will also encode the watershed location. On hovering, you would see the detailed information about the experiment and the corresponding watershed. We will also highlight a couple of interesting experiments.
